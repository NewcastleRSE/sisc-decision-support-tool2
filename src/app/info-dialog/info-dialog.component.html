Air Quality Sensor Placement Decision Support Tool

This website is part of the Spatial Inequality and the Smart City project. The website is currently under development and some features may be unavailable.

The site has two functions: data visualisation and sensor placement decision support. Users can view population characteristics and spatial data that may contribute to negative air quality hazards, exposure and vulnerability. Users can also define priority groups to view an optimal sensor placement. Currently sensor placements are available for Newcastle-upon-Tyne and data is also available for Gateshead Local Authority.

To view the source and units used for each data layer, click on the info icon. To start building your query, click on 'Optimisation Query'.
